# Assumptions & Open Questions

## Assumptions

- AppSheet free tier is sufficient for initial build; paid tier may be needed
  for email bots and scheduled automations in production
- Rank requirements (stopnie) are consistent enough to template — one set of
  RankTemplates per rank
- Managers (komisja members) will use AppSheet on phone or desktop
- Verifiers have Google accounts (needed for AppSheet sign-in)
- English is acceptable for the app UI initially (Polish labels can be added)
- The Google Form is the only intake channel (no direct AppSheet intake)
